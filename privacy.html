Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2025

TurboConvert ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Files you upload

Many TurboConvert tools process files entirely in your browser using WebAssembly — your files never leave your device and are never uploaded to our servers.

For tools that require server-side processing, uploaded files are processed immediately and permanently deleted from our servers within 1 hour. We do not store, read, share, or sell your files.

2. Data we collect automatically

When you visit TurboConvert, we collect standard server logs including:

  • IP address (anonymised)
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Referring URL

This data is used solely for analytics and service improvement. It is never sold to third parties.

3. Cookies

We use minimal cookies necessary for the site to function. We also use Google AdSense, which may place cookies for advertising purposes. You can opt out of personalised advertising at adssettings.google.com.

4. Google AdSense

TurboConvert uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies and similar technologies to show ads based on your prior visits to our site or other sites. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

5. Third-party services

We may use the following third-party services:

  • Google Analytics — anonymous traffic analysis
  • Google AdSense — advertising
  • jsDelivr / cdnjs — JavaScript libraries delivered via CDN

6. Your rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Union, you have the right to access, correct, or delete any personal data we hold about you. To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@turboconvert.io.

7. Children's privacy

TurboConvert is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The date at the top of this page reflects the latest revision. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance.

9. Contact

Questions about this policy? Email us at privacy@turboconvert.io.
