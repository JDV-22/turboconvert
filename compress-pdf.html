Why compress PDFs?

PDF files can balloon in size due to embedded fonts, high-resolution images, and metadata. A compressed PDF loads faster, attaches without issues, and takes less storage ‚Äî without losing readability.

TurboConvert runs Ghostscript directly in your browser via WebAssembly, giving you real compression ‚Äî not just a re-save.
