Why extract audio from MP4?

Extracting the audio track from a video lets you save music, podcasts, interviews, or voiceovers as standalone MP3 files ‚Äî smaller, portable, and compatible with any audio player.

TurboConvert uses FFmpeg compiled to WebAssembly to extract audio directly in your browser. No upload, no account.

Frequently asked questions

Will the audio quality change?

No quality is lost during extraction. The MP3 is encoded from the original audio stream at 192kbps by default.

Can I extract audio from any video format?

TurboConvert supports MP4, MOV, and most common video containers via FFmpeg WASM.

How long does conversion take?

Most videos convert in under 30 seconds. Longer videos may take a minute or two.

Is there a file size limit?

Files up to 500 MB are supported.
