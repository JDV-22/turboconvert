Frequently asked questions

Is TurboConvert really free?

Yes, completely. No paid plan, no daily limits, no watermarks. We're supported by non-intrusive ads.

Do you store my files?

No. Your files never leave your device. All conversion happens locally in your browser using WebAssembly.

What's the maximum file size?

100 MB for most tools, 500 MB for audio/video. There are no artificial limits beyond what your browser can handle.

Do I need to create an account?

Never. Just drop your file and convert. No email, no signup, no password.
