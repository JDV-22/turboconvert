Questions

What is a HEIC file?

HEIC (High Efficiency Image Container) is the default photo format used by iPhones since iOS 11. It produces smaller files than JPG but isn't supported by all apps and platforms.

Is my photo uploaded to a server?

Never. TurboConvert processes everything locally in your browser using the heic2any library. Your photo never leaves your device.

What if my file has the .heif extension?

TurboConvert supports both .heic and .heif files ‚Äî they are the same format with different extensions.
